My Dance Journey

West Coast Swing is a internationally known competitive and social partner dance. I have been dancing for 15 years and teaching for 5! I started when I was 7 years old in Clearwater, Florida. Just recently I am able to qualify for Champions level but I will stay an All-Star until I feel ready to move up. I only have traveled/ competed in the US but I hope to soon travel internationally in 2025!
